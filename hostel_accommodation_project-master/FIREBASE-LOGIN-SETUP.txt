## MANUAL REGISTRATION

If you want to create your own account:
1. Click "Register" tab
2. Fill in:
   - Full Name: Your Name
   - Registration Number: Any 6 digits (e.g., 111111)
   - Email: youremail@example.com
   - Gender: Select one
   - Course: Your program
   - Password: At least 6 characters
   - Confirm Password: Same as above
3. Click "Create Account"
4. Login with your credentials

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**Error: "Failed to load resource: 400"**
- The test users are being created, wait for success message
- Then try logging in

**Error: "Email already in use"**
- Good! The user exists. Just login with the credentials above

**Error: "Client is offline"**
- Disable ad blocker for localhost
- Or use incognito mode

**Still stuck?**
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- Refresh page
- Try the test credentials above
